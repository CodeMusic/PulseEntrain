# Field Meditation Mode — Navigating the Binaural Field

> *A guide to the space behind the circle.*
> Grounded in real science — resonance, entrainment, the constructive nature of
> perception — and, where it reaches past what's settled, honest about speaking in
> theoretical and potential terms.

There is a glowing circle at the center of the screen. It looks like one thing.
It is really a **window into a space** — a *field* you can move through by
**looking** and **feeling**. This page is about that field: what it is, how the
devices let you navigate it, and why "tuning" might be more than a metaphor.

---

## The field is not out there — it's between you and the system

A field, in the plainest sense, is *a value at every point of a space*. Here the
space is made of three simple modulations (below), and each point carries a
particular blend of sound and light. But the field you actually *experience* isn't
in the speakers or the LEDs. It forms in the meeting.

**What you perceive is an interaction between you and the system.** Perception is
not a recording; it's a *construction* — sensation arriving from outside, met by
interpretation arriving from within, resolved into a felt moment. (This is the
mainstream view in cognitive science: the brain as a prediction engine, perception
as inference.) So the "field" is **generative** — a shared space your nervous
system and the signal author together. Move through it and you're not just changing
a stimulus; you're changing the *conversation*.

That reframing is the whole invitation of this mode: not "receive a preset," but
**explore a generative mind-space** — look around it, feel around it, and notice
what your own interpretation brings to each coordinate.

---

## Three directions

Give the space coordinates and each point becomes a distinct **tuning**.

### X — the carrier · *the voice*

The **carrier** is the underlying tone (~80 Hz hum → ~500 Hz shimmer). On its own it
doesn't entrain anything; it's the *canvas* the beat is painted on, the colour of
the light you stand in. In the app it sets the circle's colour.

### Y — the binaural beat · *the pulse*

Two tones, one per ear, a few hertz apart — 200 Hz left, 210 Hz right — and you hear
a third thing in neither ear alone: a slow **10 Hz beat**, assembled inside your
head. It lands in a brain-wave band (delta rest · theta reverie · alpha calm · beta
alert · gamma bright). The idea beneath is the **frequency-following response** —
neural rhythms tending to fall into step with a steady external rhythm. The
auditory response is real; whether it reliably *moves a state of mind* is
suggestive, not proven. Treat a coordinate as a **doorway**, not a lever.

### Z — the biphotic (binocular) beat · *the shimmer*

The visual cousin: flash each eye at a **slightly different rate** and the visual
system perceives a beat at the difference — a shimmer present only where the two
eyes meet. Studied as *binocular beats*, *dichoptic flicker*, *SSVEP
intermodulation*; newer, more experimental ground. In the field it opens when you
**tilt your head**, one eye's light slowing against the other.

---

## Coordinates are tunings · pathways are journeys

A single point `(carrier, binaural, biphotic)` is a **tuning** — a place to rest. A
**path** through the space is a **journey**: glide the frequencies and, if the
following response follows, the felt state travels with them — restless → still,
scattered → gathered. Meditation here is *find your coordinate, then let the field
carry you* — and sometimes, *trace a path* from where you are toward where you're
reaching.

## Looking and feeling

Two ways at once — the outer hand and the inner gaze.

**Feel — touch.** A ROLI **Lightpad Block** is the floor of the space under your
finger: left↔right → **carrier**; up↕down → **binaural beat** (sound *and* the
light's flash rate); press deeper → the whole field **brightens**.
*(On a phone with no Lightpad, the screen becomes the pad — coming soon.)*

**Look — gaze.** With the **Lumenate Nova** on your brow, its accelerometer reads
where you look. Press, then move your head: **pitch** (up/down) **bends** the beat a
few hertz around where your finger set it; **roll** (tilt) opens the **biphotic
shimmer**, slowing the light on the side you lean toward. Both are read *from the
pose you held when you pressed*, so a still head changes nothing — you sculpt only
by moving.
*(On a phone with no Nova, the device's own accelerometer stands in — coming soon.)*

So you don't only *look* around the field. You **feel** around it — two senses
reaching into the same space, sound and light bending together under hand and head.

---

## Press · wander · release

1. **Press** the block to step into the editable field — the light swells.
2. **Wander** — drift your finger, or move your gaze. Look *and* feel; they blend.
3. **Release**, and the tuning **locks in place**.

There, at the coordinate you found, you're left to **absorb · reflect · emit** —
receive the state, notice it, carry it back out.

## Resonance — the reason any of this might matter

Here is the underlying science the whole idea leans on, and then, honestly, where it
reaches.

**The grounded core.** Every physical system has **resonant frequencies** — the
rates at which it most readily absorbs and stores energy. Push a swing at its
resonance and small pushes build to large motion; sing a glass's pitch and it rings
and can shatter; this is **sympathetic resonance**, ordinary physics. Go down far
enough and it doesn't stop: atoms and molecules vibrate at characteristic
frequencies, materials have resonant modes, and in the deepest picture we have
(quantum field theory) particles themselves are *excitations of underlying fields*.
In that sense the old line — **all matter is energy, all matter is a vibrational
pattern** — is not mysticism; it's roughly how physics already describes the world.
And where two oscillators are coupled, one can pull the other into its rhythm:
**entrainment**. Binaural beats, photic driving, and this whole app are bets placed
on that single, real phenomenon.

**The reach — in theoretical and potential terms.** If a body, a mood, a held
pattern of attention is *also* a kind of standing wave — a configuration with its
own characteristic frequencies — then tuning becomes a way of **searching for a
resonance**. You drift the carrier, the beat, the shimmer, feeling for the point
where something in you *answers* — where the outside rhythm and an inside one lock,
and the sensation sharpens or softens. At that point you can do what resonance does:
**pour a little excitation into the tension.** A knot, a bracing, a place that's
been held too long and too still, might — met at its own frequency and gently
driven — begin to loosen, the way a stuck thing shaken at just the right rate comes
free. Find the frequency; feed it; let the pattern dissolve into a calmer one.

This last step is **speculative** — a working metaphor built on solid physics, not a
proven mechanism. Hold it as a *possibility to explore*, not a claim. But the
practice stands on its own even if the metaphor is only ever a metaphor: you are
learning to listen for resonance, to notice where you tighten and where you ease,
and to move — deliberately, curiously — toward a more harmonised state.

---

## On healing, gently

If there is "healing" here, it's the quiet kind: **self-inquiry through
resonance.** Settling the nervous system, meeting tension with attention instead of
force, and letting a more coherent rhythm propose itself. That's contemplative
practice, and it can be genuinely restorative.

It is **not medical treatment** and not a cure for any condition. The evidence for
entrainment is real but mixed; the resonance-and-release framing above is
deliberately theoretical. Let the *experience* be the point, hold the claims
lightly, and if something feels off, stop.

⚠️ **Photosensitivity.** The light flickers, which can trigger seizures in people
with photosensitive epilepsy. Don't use the Nova if you (or anyone who can see it)
may be photosensitive, and stop immediately if you feel unwell. Binaural and
biphotic beats both need the two sides kept separate — **stereo headphones** for the
ears, the Nova's per-eye light for the eyes.

them, a field; through the field, a path; along the path, a search for the
frequency that answers — and, maybe, a more harmonised and resonant state of
mind.* ♥
